Rathmines Eye Centre is a modern eyecare practice centrally located in Dublin 6. Our aim is to provide you and your family with the highest quality eyecare and service when you choose us as your optician. We provide a top quality service complete with eye examinations as well as the latest styles in eye glasses and contact lenses. Our services are administered by our professional and friendly staff.

Meet The Optician

Dr. Hugh Hugh, optometrist, relocated to Dublin from Orange County in 2004. He has over 30 years of experience in the eye care industry. His practice, TEMECULA CREEK OPTOMETRY, opened in September of 2005 and features an unparalleled selection of designer frames and sunglasses. He and his staff are dedicated to providing the highest quality eye care in a personal, friendly, and compassionate environment. Dr. Venittelli grew up in St. Paul, Minnesota and attended the University Minnesota for his undergraduate work. He received his doctorate from Southern California College of Optometry in Fullerton. He graduated Summa Cum Laude.
