Rathmines Eye Centre is a modern eyecare practice centrally located in Dublin 6. Our aim is to provide you and your family with the highest quality eyecare and service when you choose us as your optician. We provide a top quality service complete with eye examinations as well as the latest styles in eye glasses and contact lenses. Our services are administered by our professional and friendly staff.
